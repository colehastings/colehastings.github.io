My Youtube Channel


Before you dive into my videos, I'll give you a little background on my YouTube channel, and why I started it.


    I've always enjoyed YouTube. For the majority of the time I've spent on Youtube throughout my life, I would watch gaming videos, music videos, and other nonsense that gave me instant gratification to pass the time. But around 6 months ago, I realized just how powerful the platform of Youtube really is.


    You can learn (virtually) anything on YouTube. How to tie a tie, how to jump start a car battery, how to code (I learned how to do most of this website from YouTube), etc. I knew this, but had never thought to start searching for topics to grow myself, and my well being. I still don't know what prompted that initial start to Personal Development,but once I found my first videos on confidence, meditation, and developing a "growth mindset", I was instantly hooked and hungry for more. These past 6 months have been an incredibly enlightening and engaging experience as I continue to delve deeper into my subconscious mind and my true abilities.


    Even before I was learning from YouTube and instead using it as a way to procrastinate, I had always wanted to post my own videos. My problem? I never had the motivation and thought I lacked the skill neccessary to develop quality content. After I changed my mindset and habits to ones that would help me grow myself for the rest of my life, I realized that these were just excuses. With a little dedication to learning the creator side of YouTube and the editing software, I could produce and share all of the ideas and lessons I've learned on this journey to my friends, family, or anyone trying to genuinely better themselves.


    So without further ado, here are my videos (with the newest at the top, and the oldest at the bottom). My channel is based solely around Personal Development, and I hope YOU can develop some new habits or mindsets from these videos, just as I have too.
